‚ö†Ô∏è Key Bottlenecks Identified

1. Inspector Dependency: Even with smart scan cars, Parkin had to hire 31 additional drivers in Sept 2025 and redeploy vehicle operators to field roles ‚Äî a 10% effective headcount increase just to keep up with demand.
2. Road-Only Coverage: Scan cars can only cover road-accessible zones. Off-street lots, multi-storey facilities, and tight urban areas remain manual-inspection dependent.
3. Temporal Gaps: Enforcement operates on shift schedules. Violations between shifts, late night, and early morning go undetected.
4. Scaling Costs: Each new zone requires proportional inspector/vehicle deployment. Linear cost scaling doesn't match Parkin's exponential growth trajectory.

Autonomous Drone Enforcement Platform

A network of self-deploying drones operating from compact docking stations, providing continuous aerial parking enforcement across all zone types.

üõ©Ô∏è AeraScan Drone

üß† AeraScan Cloud

üì± AeraScan Ops

Operator dashboard for Parkin's enforcement team. Live drone tracking, zone heatmaps, violation alerts, evidence review queue, and one-click fine issuance. Integrates with existing Parkin enforcement workflows and handheld devices.

How It Works

1. Auto-Deploy

Drones launch from docks on scheduled missions or real-time demand triggers. Each dock covers a 2km radius. No human intervention required.

2. Aerial Scan

Drone flies patrol routes at 30-50m altitude, scanning plates from above at 12 m/s. Covers parking rows inaccessible to ground vehicles. Reads plates in <100ms.

3. Instant Enforce

Each plate is cross-referenced against Parkin's payment system in real-time. Violations are auto-flagged with evidence package: photo, plate number, GPS coordinates, timestamp.

09 ¬∑ Regulatory & Compliance

UAE Drone Operations Framework

GCAA Compliance

All operations under UAE General Civil Aviation Authority regulations. Remote Pilot License (RPL) for supervisors, RPAS operator certificate for commercial operations, and airspace coordination with Dubai Civil Aviation Authority for urban BVLOS flights.

Privacy & Data

Camera systems capture only license plates ‚Äî no facial recognition, no personal identification. All data processed and stored in UAE-based cloud infrastructure. Compliant with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45/2021 on personal data protection.

Safety Systems

Redundant flight controllers, automated return-to-dock on low battery or signal loss, geo-fencing to prevent zone departure, parachute recovery system for emergency descent. Full compliance with SORA risk assessment methodology.

Insurance

Comprehensive third-party liability coverage for all drone operations. Hull insurance on all airframes. Cyber insurance on data platform. All policies compliant with GCAA minimum requirements for commercial RPAS operations.
